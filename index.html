What is the VariKey?

The VariKey is an innovative, easy to use, multi-functional handheld cybersecurity testing device. It is capable of tracking both Bluetooth, and Wi-Fi signals from up to 30 meters away, as well as running a multitude of security protocols to test Wi-Fi strength and identifying possible backdoors and vulnerable weak points in a network. 
ESP32U Microcontroller

Housing an ESP32U microcontroller, this board is capable of operating at 240 MHz all while keeping a clean minimalistic profile. 

3.2 Inch TFT LCD 

A 3.2 inch LCD provides the user with high image quality and an array of colors. This module allows the operator of the VariKey to easily view vital information.  

4x4 Digital Keypad

A simplistic module, however a vital component of the VariKey. This digital keypad allows for easy maneuvering through the VariKey's custom firmware. 

Is your network safe?

Hackers can have backdoor access to your networks without you even realizing, allowing for both personal data, and sensitive info, to be leaked publicly onto the internet. The VariKey provides a way to test and ensure a Wi-Fi network is free of these backdoor entrances and points of weak security.

Why the VariKey?

Ease of Use

The VariKey is built to have a simplistic design, allowing for a user-friendly experience. As well as a simple custom firmware called vkOS, coded for easy navigation around the VariKey's built-in cybersecurity protocols.   

A Tool in Your Arsenal

Bypass the hassle of having to hire or install heaps of cybersecurity bloatware, and take matters into your own hands. We provide you with the one tool that can effectively assess points of weak security.

Custom Firmware

Current cybersecurity tools often have complex firmware's that are difficult to navigate. VariKey's custom vkOS firmware is designed for simplicity all while housing numerous cybersecurity software and protocols, which are extremely effective at what they do.
